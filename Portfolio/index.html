Lo Gwin

Creative Services Executive/ Financial Professional

Beautiful Sunrise! I am Lo. I am an 24 year old artist, an an activist and an entrepenuer. I found peace and love in creating art, in all of its forms. I dabble in each category to see what skills I can learn and grow over time. My goal as an artist is to create with all different mediums, try new things, and just flow within art itself. I think in each of it's forms there's a lesson to be learned. In painting there's patience, music it's listening, photography is stillness, in web design it's trying not to cry. In each form art is a powerfful teacher, and I am a student of life!
